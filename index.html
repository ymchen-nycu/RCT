國立陽明交通大學可靠通訊技術實驗室（RCT LAB）
指導教授：陳彥銘 教授

可靠通訊技術實驗室位於工程四館 ED903，專注於推動下世代通訊技術的研究與創新。

研究領域

  1. 新世代基頻無線通訊技術──非正交多重接取 (NOMA, Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access)、整合感測與通訊 (ISAC, Integrated Sensing and Communication)、語義通訊 (Semantic Communication)、正交時頻空間 (OTFS, Orthogonal Time-Frequency Space)。
  2. 編碼與調變──錯誤更正碼 (ECC，Error Correcting Codes)、聯合源通道編碼(JSCC , Joint Source-Channel Coding)、編碼調變 (Coded Modulation)。
  3. 資訊安全──生物辨識加密系統 (Biometric Cryptosystems) 及相關研究。

實驗室理念

我們重視開放與自我成長，並積極在學術界與產業界創造創新與貢獻。

誠摯招募

我們正在尋找積極進取、充滿熱情的碩博士新生！
若您對 RCT LAB 的研究領域有興趣，誠摯歡迎與我們聯繫，一同加入研究行列！

Reliable Communication Technology Laboratory (RCT LAB)
National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University
Advisor: Prof. Yen-Ming Chen

The Reliable Communication Technology Laboratory (RCT LAB), located in Engineering Building IV, Room ED903, is dedicated to advancing next-generation communication technologies.

Research Areas

  1. Next-Generation Baseband Wireless Communication Technologies – Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA), Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC), Semantic Communication, Orthogonal Time-Frequency Space (OTFS).
  2. Coding and Modulation – Error Correcting Codes (ECC), Joint Source-Channel Coding (JSCC), Coded Modulation.
  3. Information Security – Biometric Cryptosystems and related topics.

Our Vision

We foster an open and self-driven research culture, aiming to bring innovative contributions to both academia and industry.

Join Us!

We are actively seeking motivated and passionate master's and doctoral students. If you are interested in our research topics, we warmly welcome you to contact us and become part of RCT LAB.
